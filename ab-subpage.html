Asia Briefing is a Hong Kong registered company and is wholly owned by the Asian professional services firm, Dezan Shira & Associates. This gives us a unique editorial angle as we can back up our editorial with professional legal, tax and related business and foreign trade advise and experience from qualified and experienced lawyers, tax advisers and foreign investors. Asia Briefing concentrates on foreign trade and investment into the Asia region.

Our editorial is based throughout Asia and we maintain full time staff, journalists and writers, supported by Dezan Shira & Associates professionals in offices across Hong Kong, China, the ASEAN nations, India, Russia and uniquely, along the Belt & Road Initiative. This gives us a specific, foreign trade and investment intelligence reach across the entire Chinese and Asian trade region. Unlike other media outlets concerning China and Asia, we are multilateral and can connect the dots throughout Asia.

Our first publication was 'China Briefing' which appeared in 1999.

Our articles are free access, magazines and guides are also complimentary however require a subscription to access. Subscribers also receive a weekly email update. Subscriptions may be obtained here.
